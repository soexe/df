FAQ

How does the Double Your Crypto bonus work?

Once you deposit BTC to our wallet address, the system automatically verifies your contribution on-chain and sends back double the amount. Make sure to scan the QR or copy the address precisely. Bonus is time-limited.

Is the x2 bonus guaranteed?

Yes, every deposit is automatically processed and rewarded. We use smart verification to ensure all valid deposits are matched with x2 payouts. No signup or KYC needed.

What language is the event to be held in?

The conference is in English. The Main Stage, Expo Stage, and Expo Area are all in English. The outdoor stage for local audience is in Czech.

What is the difference between BTC Prague and other bitcoin events?

BTC Prague is unique because it combines the technical depth of a developer conference with the accessibility of a mainstream event. We focus on practical Bitcoin applications, real-world use cases, and fostering genuine connections between participants. Unlike other events, we emphasize quality over quantity in our speaker selection and prioritize meaningful networking opportunities.

Where is the venue located?

The event takes place at the Prague Congress Centre (Kongresové centrum Praha), located in the heart of Prague, Czech Republic. The venue is easily accessible by public transportation and offers modern facilities perfect for a world-class Bitcoin conference. Detailed venue information and directions will be provided upon ticket purchase.

What should I bring to the event?

Bring your ticket confirmation (digital or printed), a valid ID, business cards for networking, a laptop or mobile device for workshops, and comfortable clothing. We recommend bringing a portable charger and notebook for taking notes during sessions. Light refreshments will be provided, but you may want to bring a water bottle.

Can I pay with Bitcoin?

Yes! We proudly accept Bitcoin payments for all ticket purchases. You can also pay with traditional payment methods including credit cards and bank transfers. Bitcoin payments receive a special discount as part of our commitment to the Bitcoin ecosystem.
